Birthday Countdown Timer

Fill up the form on right side to start the timer showing the remaining time to the specified birthday. Upon hitting zero, a buzzer will start to buzz reminding you to wish the person.

Steps:

  1. Enter the date (1-31)
  2. Enter the month (1-12)
  3. Enter the year
  4. Click on the Start Timer button
